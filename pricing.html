What is CMS??

CMS (content management system) is a nice and easy way for you to be able to edit certain parts of your website (like text and images) while not having to ask me to edit the actual code once the website is finished. I’ll be using a CMS service named October CMS. You don’t have to get your website with a CMS service installed on it. However, if you don’t get the CMS plan and later you want to edit parts of your site, like the text or images, then you will have to contact me and tell me exactly what you want changed. I’d be more than happy to edit the site for you, but I will charge $50 per hour I have to spend on your site after it is already finished and published.
